About

they're tracking us, unplug, unplug

Hi, I'm Giuslock: a penetration tester with 5 years of experience across web apps, mobile, and cloud.

I have a deep fascination for geopolitics and tech. Why? Because data is the new raw material everyone is hunting for, and the line between global conflicts and cyber security is thinner than ever.

When I'm not doing my day job, I'm hunting bugs on HackerOne, playing CTFs on HackTheBox, or studying for my next challenge (currently holding CPTS and BSCP).

What to expect from this blog?

No strict rules. I write about whatever catches my attention. You'll find CTF write-ups, my personal reviews on cybersecurity certifications, deep dives into OSINT and AI, or just my thoughts on what's happening around the globe.

It's a chaotic mix, but basically, I'm just documenting my journey through these crazy times.
